Our approach to

safety

We recognize that every time you open your HostTrans app, you're putting your trust in our technology—to not only connect you with a driver, but to also give you tools in case of emergency. That trust is what drives us to continuously raise the bar, building new safety features, setting guidelines for respectful and positive experiences, and more.
Whether you’re a rider, driver, or anyone who uses HostTrans, your safety drives us.
